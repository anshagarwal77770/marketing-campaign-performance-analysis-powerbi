
# 📊 Marketing Campaign Performance Analysis | Power BI

## 📌 Overview

This project presents an end-to-end **Marketing Campaign Performance Analysis** dashboard built using **Power BI**. The dashboard evaluates campaign effectiveness across multiple marketing channels by analyzing ROI, conversion rates, customer acquisition cost (CPA), lead generation, revenue, and budget allocation. The objective is to identify high-performing marketing channels and provide data-driven recommendations for optimizing marketing investments. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Identify the most effective marketing campaigns.
- Evaluate ROI across different marketing channels.
- Analyze conversion rates and customer acquisition costs (CPA).
- Optimize marketing budget allocation.
- Recommend strategies to maximize campaign performance and profitability.

## 📈 Key Insights

- Influencer marketing generated the highest revenue but required the largest budget.
- Email marketing delivered the lowest Cost Per Acquisition (CPA), making it the most cost-efficient channel.
- Event campaigns achieved the highest conversion rate but had the highest acquisition cost.
- Social Media generated the highest number of leads but recorded the lowest conversion efficiency.
- Revenue consistently peaked during October–December, while July showed the weakest performance across channels.
- Budget optimization suggested increasing investment in Email and Influencer campaigns while reducing Event marketing spend. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Increase investment in Email campaigns due to their exceptional cost efficiency.
- Maintain Influencer marketing as a primary revenue-generating channel.
- Use Social Media primarily for awareness and lead generation.
- Reduce Event marketing spend and focus only on high-performing campaigns.
- Allocate a larger portion of the annual budget during the October–December festive season for maximum ROI. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}
